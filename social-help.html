• Find friends who use Social Calendar by searching for them by name/email in the friends tab!
  • You can share various filters and their respective events with friends by including them in the "share with" section when you create or edit a filter. That way, the appropriate people will see events on your calender. 
  • You can compare various filters with friends by pressing the two crossed arrows icon in calender view. This way, events that overlap will become apparent. 
  • Follow your favorite celebrities on Social Calendar! Search for them by name in the "friends" section, and get the latest events they have shared!
